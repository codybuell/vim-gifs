Navigation
==========

0.  hjkl -> left, down, up, right (respectively)
1.  0 $  -> move to beginning or end of line (respectively)
2.  C-f  -> page *FORWARD*
3.  C-b  -> page *BACKWARD*
4.  S-h  -> go *HIGH*
5.  S-m  -> go *MIDDLE*
6.  S-l  -> go *LOW*
7.  w    -> go to next *WORD*
8.  b    -> go *BACK* one word
9.  e    -> to to *END* of word
10. gg   -> go to top of the document
11. S-g  -> go to the bottom of the document
12. f(x) -> *FIND* next occurence of (x) on current line
